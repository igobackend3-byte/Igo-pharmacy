Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

This document is published for transparency and may be revised periodically. If you have any questions about how it applies to you, contact our team at bd2@igogroups.com.

1. Information We Collect

When you use this website or contact us through the enquiry form, WhatsApp, phone or email, we may collect: your name, phone number, email address, city/state, and details of the products or services you're enquiring about. If you use the shopping cart, item selections are stored locally in your browser only, and are not transmitted to us until you choose to send an enquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your product or service enquiries
  • To recommend products matched to your crop, land or livestock
  • To process dealer/distributor applications
  • To send occasional seasonal advisory content, if you've subscribed to our newsletter

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We may share your information with logistics/dispatch partners solely to fulfil an order you've confirmed with us, and with government scheme portals only where you've explicitly asked for help applying to one (e.g. Soil Health Card testing).

4. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and order-related information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide support and comply with applicable tax/business record-keeping requirements.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the details below.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email precisionfarming152@gmail.com or call +91 73977 89803.

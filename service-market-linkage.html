Market Linkage Algorithm
  1. INPUT: crop, grade, quantity, farmer location
  2. LOOKUP nearby mandi options + general reference pricing
  3. IF e-NAM eligible → point to APMC registration path
  4. NOTE: we do not broker, trade, or guarantee price — awareness only
  5. OUTPUT: farmer makes an informed decision and sells independently
Before You Ask

What to have ready for useful market pointers.

  • Crop, grade and quantity — quality/grade affects the price range you should expect, so have this ready, not just the crop name.
  • Your location — mandi options and prices are local, so we need your district/nearest market to point you correctly.
  • e-NAM registration — to sell on e-NAM (National Agriculture Market) you need to be registered with your local APMC mandi and hold a valid mandi licence/registration; we can explain the basics, but registration itself happens through your APMC.
  • What we don't do — we're not a broker or trading platform; we share general price-checking and mandi awareness, the actual sale and negotiation is always yours.

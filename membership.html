Growth Journey

Grow From Farmer to Agri-Entrepreneur

Five IGO Pharmacy farmer support tiers — not paid subscriptions, just the right level of advisory, government-scheme awareness, and growth support matched to your farm size and how you farm.

Traditional, hands-on farming
01
Traditional / Small Farmer

Classic Farmer

0.5–2 hectare land, traditional farming methods, limited technology adoption.

Ask About This Tier
Farmer Profile
  • 0.5–2 hectare land
  • Traditional farming methods
  • Limited mechanization/tech use
Govt. Schemes to Know
  • PM-KISAN income support
  • PMFBY crop insurance
  • Soil Health Card Scheme
  • State input subsidy schemes
Informational only — IGO Pharmacy doesn't administer these.
IGO Support
  • Basic crop advisory over WhatsApp/phone
  • Help reading your Soil Health Card
  • Low-cost input kits sized to small plots
  • Seasonal sowing/spraying reminders
Growth Support
  • Village-level demo visits (case-by-case)
  • Local-language WhatsApp advisory
  • Nearby sourcing convenience
Next Step

Move up to Modern Farmer as you adopt drip irrigation, better seed varieties, or start selling surplus beyond your village.

Drip irrigation, better inputs
02
Progressive / Semi-Tech Farmer

Modern Farmer

2–5 hectare land, uses drip irrigation or improved seed varieties, market-oriented.

Ask About This Tier
Farmer Profile
  • 2–5 hectare land
  • Uses drip/improved seed varieties
  • Market-oriented, sells surplus
Govt. Schemes to Know
  • PMKSY (micro-irrigation subsidy)
  • Mechanization subsidy schemes
  • PKVY organic farming subsidy
  • PM-KUSUM solar pump scheme
Informational only — IGO Pharmacy doesn't administer these.
IGO Support
  • Season-long input sequencing advisory
  • Product recommendations for yield goals
  • Weather-linked spraying pointers
  • Priority WhatsApp response
Growth Support
  • Nearby mandi/buyer awareness pointers
  • Basic grading tips for better sale price
  • Help exploring FPO membership
Next Step

Move up to Innovative Farmer as you scale toward cluster farming, cold storage, or export-quality produce.

Most Popular
Mechanized, export-ready farming
03
Tech + High-Value Farming

Innovative Farmer

5–10 hectare or farmer cluster, early tech adopter, export/processing focus.

Ask About This Tier
Farmer Profile
  • 5–10 hectare / cluster
  • Smart farming adopter
  • Export/processing focus
Govt. Schemes to Know
  • Agriculture Infrastructure Fund
  • Cold storage subsidy schemes
  • APEDA export awareness
  • State precision-farming grants
Informational only — IGO Pharmacy doesn't administer these.
IGO Support
  • Post-harvest handling guidance
  • Storage & warehousing signposting
  • Export-readiness awareness (IEC/APEDA basics)
  • Equipment rental enquiry routing
We don't operate our own warehouse or trading house.
Growth Support
  • Positioning tips for premium produce
  • B2B buyer-awareness pointers
  • Basic traceability guidance
Next Step

Move up to Entrepreneur Farmer if you're ready to build a processing unit or your own dealer/franchise business.

Processing & a second income stream
04
Farmer + Business Owner

Entrepreneur Farmer

Business-minded, farming-plus-processing focus, ready for a second income stream.

Ask About This Tier
Farmer Profile
  • Business mindset
  • Processing / storage / services focus
  • Ready to invest in a second income stream
Govt. Schemes to Know
  • MSME / Udyam registration
  • Startup India (agri-tech ventures)
  • Agriculture Infrastructure Fund
  • Credit Guarantee schemes
Informational only — IGO Pharmacy doesn't administer these.
IGO Support
  • Business-planning conversation with our team
  • Dealer/franchise partnership pathway
  • Bulk sourcing support
  • Direct point of contact, no ticket queue
Growth Support
  • Packaging/branding basics
  • B2B and retail customer-mix guidance
  • Introductions where relevant
Next Step

Apply to become an IGO Pharmacy Dealer — see the full franchise application.

Wholesale & mandi trading
05
Trader / Aggregator / Buyer

Merchant Farmer

Trader, wholesaler, or aggregator working across multiple farms and villages.

Ask About This Tier
Farmer Profile
  • Trader / wholesaler
  • Aggregation across villages
  • Market-price aware
Govt. Schemes to Know
  • WDRA warehouse registration info
  • Trade infrastructure schemes
  • Export-import (IEC) awareness
Informational only — IGO Pharmacy doesn't administer these.
IGO Support
  • Bulk/wholesale sourcing enquiries
  • Supply-chain coordination conversations
  • Priority account contact
Growth Support
  • Market-intelligence pointers (Agmarknet, e-NAM)
  • Digital record-keeping tips
  • Buyer-network introductions where relevant

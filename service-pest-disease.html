Triage Algorithm
  1. INPUT: crop, symptom description, close-up photo of affected leaf/plant
  2. IF photo shows a recognizable pattern → match to likely pest/disease
  3. ELSE → agronomist asks a follow-up question (leaf position, weather, recent spray history)
  4. MATCH confirmed cause → recommend a specific crop-protection product and dosage
  5. OUTPUT: product recommendation + option to follow up if symptoms persist
Before You Send a Photo

What to share for a faster, more accurate answer.

  • Crop and growth stage — helps narrow down which pests/diseases are likely at this point in the season.
  • A clear, close-up photo — of the affected leaf, stem, or fruit, in daylight if possible. A blurry or distant photo slows down an accurate read.
  • When it started and how it's spreading — a single leaf vs. the whole field points to very different causes.
  • What's already been sprayed — any recent pesticide/fungicide application, so we don't recommend something you've already tried or something that conflicts.

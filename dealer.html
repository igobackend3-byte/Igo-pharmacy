Stockist Onboarding Algorithm

Before You Apply

What to have ready for the onboarding conversation.

  • A retail or storage space — owned or rented, in a location with footfall for wellness/health products; exact area needed is sized to your plan during onboarding.
  • ID and address proof — for the individual or business entity applying.
  • Basic capital for first stock — the investment figure isn't fixed company-wide since it depends on your location and product mix; we walk through this with you directly in Step 3 rather than quoting a number that wouldn't be accurate for your case.
  • Local market knowledge — familiarity with customers in your area helps us size stock and support correctly.
  • Time commitment — this is a hands-on retail role, not a passive investment.

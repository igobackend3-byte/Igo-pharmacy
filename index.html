Growth Journey

IGO Membership Levels.

Five farmer support tiers — not paid subscriptions, just the right level of advisory, govt. scheme awareness, and support matched to how you farm.

Tier 01

Classic Farmer

0.5–2 hectare, traditional farming. Basic advisory, soil testing guidance, low-cost input kits.

Tier 02

Modern Farmer

2–5 hectare, market-oriented. Weather-based advisory, input subscription support, buyer linkage.

Tier 03

Innovative Farmer

5–10 hectare / cluster. Post-harvest management, cold storage guidance, export linkage awareness.

Tier 04

Entrepreneur Farmer

Business-minded, processing focus. Business planning support, franchise ownership, investor connects.

Tier 05

Merchant Farmer

Trader / wholesaler / aggregator. Supply chain awareness, market intelligence, digital trade pointers.

Yes — we dispatch pan-India, leveraging IGO's existing logistics network. Delivery timelines vary by location.

Are your products genuine and certified?

All products are sourced directly from certified, brand-authorized manufacturers with quality checks before dispatch.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Minimums vary by product and category. Send an enquiry with your requirement and we'll confirm pricing and quantity.

Can I get expert advice on a crop problem?

Yes — message us on WhatsApp or use the "Find Solutions" tool above and our team will advise on the right product.

Visit our Become a Dealer page and submit your details — our team will follow up directly. There is currently no automated approval or online dealer portal.

Who We Are

Bringing Traditional Siddha & Ayurveda Wellness to Every Home

IGO Pharmacy is the upcoming herbal wellness brand from IGO Group of Companies, based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu — built to offer traditional Siddha & Ayurveda formulations with traceable sourcing and honest, practitioner-guided advice.

Our Identity

A traceable, quality-first approach to herbal wellness.

IGO Pharmacy exists to solve a simple, real problem: people want traditional Siddha & Ayurveda formulations they can actually trust — where they come from, how they're made, and whether they're genuine. We're building a focused range of churnam, kashayam, thailam, tablets and personal care products, sourced with farm-to-pharmacy traceability in mind.

We're not yet operational — this site reflects our honest, in-progress roadmap. Every enquiry reaches our own team directly over WhatsApp or phone, so you can ask questions, register interest, and be the first to know when the range launches.

Institutional Framework

Core Pillars

Traditional Formulations

Churnam, kashayam, thailam & tablets rooted in classical Siddha & Ayurveda practice.

Traceable Sourcing

Farm-to-pharmacy visibility on ingredients and batches, once operational.

Practitioner Guidance

WhatsApp-based consultation with Siddha/Ayurveda practitioners once live.

Growing Partner Network

The North Star

Our Vision

To be a trusted, direct line between people and genuine Siddha & Ayurveda wellness — reducing guesswork, and the risk of unreliable products, in every purchase.

The Mission Blueprint

Operational Mission

  • Offer traditional, traceable herbal formulations at fair prices
  • Provide honest, practitioner-backed guidance — not just sales
  • Build toward GMP-certified, quality-first manufacturing
  • Grow a stockist/distributor network that extends this reach further
🌿 Launch-stage transparency: IGO Pharmacy's herbal range is not yet operational. Every figure and claim on this site reflects our real, in-progress status — we don't fabricate certifications, prices or availability. Message us on WhatsApp to be notified when we go live.
The Bigger Picture

Part of the IGO Group of Companies

IGO Pharmacy is one vertical of the IGO Group — a pan-India agricultural and wellness ecosystem headquartered in Chennai. The Group's vision is to be the leading pan-India brand in precision agriculture and agri engineering, and IGO Pharmacy extends that same traceability-first philosophy into herbal wellness.
